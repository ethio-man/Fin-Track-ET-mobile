## Project Overview

**FinTrack Mobile** is a comprehensive mobile application designed to help individuals and businesses seamlessly manage their finances, track sales, monitor expenses, and maintain inventory. Built with modern mobile development frameworks, the app provides a user-friendly interface with data visualization and robust financial tracking capabilities.

## Technology Stack

- **Framework**: React Native with Expo
- **Navigation**: React Navigation (Stack and Bottom Tabs)
- **UI & Graphics**: React Native Chart Kit, React Native SVG, Expo Linear Gradient
- **Native Integrations**: Expo Haptics, Expo Image Picker, Expo Print, Expo Sharing, Expo Splash Screen

---

## Main Functionalities

### 1. User Authentication

Secure login and registration flows to ensure user data is protected and personalized to each individual or business owner.

### 2. Dashboard & Overview

A centralized home screen/dashboard that provides a quick glance at the overall financial health, including key metrics, recent activities, and charts summarizing income versus expenses.

### 3. Sales Tracking

Allows users to record, categorize, and monitor sales transactions. It provides a straightforward interface to log daily revenue and track performance over time.

### 4. Expense Management

Provides functionality to input and categorize business or personal expenses. Helps users keep their costs in check and analyze where their money is going.

### 5. Inventory Management

A dedicated module to manage products, track stock levels, and update inventory statuses. It ensures users always know what items are available, low in stock, or out of stock.

### 6. Invoice Generation & Sharing

Enables users to generate professional invoices for their sales or services. With integration to Expo Print and Sharing, users can easily export and send invoices directly to clients.

### 7. Debt Tracking

A specialized feature to manage accounts receivable and payable. Users can track who owes them money and who they owe, complete with due dates and partial payment tracking.

### 8. Business Reports & Analytics

Generates detailed financial reports using interactive charts and graphs (via React Native Chart Kit). It gives users actionable insights into profitability, cash flow, and business trends.

### 9. Business Details & Configuration

Allows users to set up their specific business profiles, configure taxation rules, currency, and other critical business details required for accurate financial management.

### 10. User Profile & Settings

A customizable settings area where users can manage their personal profiles, update app preferences, toggle themes or notifications, and access additional options.
